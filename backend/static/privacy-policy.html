FlexInventory Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

1. Introduction

FlexInventory ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Store Information

  • Shopify store domain and store name
  • Store owner contact information (name, email)
  • Store settings (currency, timezone, location)
  • Billing and subscription information

2.2 Product and Inventory Data

  • Product information (titles, descriptions, SKUs, variants)
  • Inventory levels and stock movements
  • Custom fields and metadata you create
  • Supplier and vendor information
  • Purchase orders and cost data

2.3 Usage and Analytics Data

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Performance metrics and error logs
  • IP addresses and browser information
  • Session data and access timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Provide inventory management and tracking services
  • Generate forecasts and analytics
  • Send alerts and notifications
  • Sync inventory across multiple channels
  • Process custom workflows and automation

3.2 Business Operations

  • Process billing and subscription management
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

4.2 We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing

4.3 Third-Party Integrations

If you choose to connect FlexInventory with other services (Amazon, eBay, etc.), we will share relevant inventory data as necessary to provide the integration service. You control these integrations and can disconnect them at any time.

5. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.2+) for data transmission
  • All data is stored in secure, encrypted databases
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly monitor for security vulnerabilities
  • We implement backup and disaster recovery procedures

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • After account cancellation, data is retained for 90 days to allow reactivation
  • Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance
  • You can request data deletion at any time (subject to legal requirements)

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Access and Portability

  • View and download your data through the app interface
  • Export your data in common formats (CSV, JSON)
  • Request a copy of all data we have about you

7.2 Data Correction and Deletion

  • Update your information through the app settings
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data

7.3 Communication Preferences

  • Control email notifications and alerts
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Manage webhook and API notifications

8. Cookies and Tracking

  • We use essential cookies for app functionality
  • Analytics cookies help us improve the service
  • You can control cookies through your browser settings
  • Some features may not work without certain cookies

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Other legally approved transfer mechanisms

10. Children's Privacy

FlexInventory is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Email notification to your registered email address
  • Notice within the FlexInventory app
  • Posting the updated policy on our website

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your personal information includes:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide our services
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving our services and preventing fraud
  • Consent: Marketing communications and optional features
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws
